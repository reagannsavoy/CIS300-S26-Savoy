Sustainable Shopping in Action

Buying secondhand and extending the life of clothing helps reduce textile waste, conserve resources, and support a more sustainable fashion industry.

Buying Tips

Check fabric labels, look closely for stains or damage, and choose pieces you can style more than one way.

Selling Tips

Take clear photos, write honest descriptions, include measurements, and price items fairly.

Where to Shop

Try local thrift stores, vintage shops, Poshmark, Depop, eBay, and TheRealReal.
